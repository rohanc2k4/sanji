# MATH401 final exam scope

Linear algebra final, comprehensive. The four topics that have shown up on every past exam:

1. **Singular value decomposition** — see [[svd-notes]] for the working notes. Eckart-Young, low-rank approximation, and the connection to PCA are the load-bearing pieces.
2. **Eigendecomposition** — characteristic polynomial, diagonalization, when it fails (defective matrices).
3. **Orthogonal projections** — least squares as a projection onto column space; the normal equations.
4. **Inner-product geometry** — Gram-Schmidt, QR decomposition.
