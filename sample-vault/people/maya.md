Frontend engineer, friend from college. Reviewing UX work on the [[overview|sanji-vault-demo]] and the v0.1 launch in general.

## Strong opinions

- Onboarding flows should never have a Skip button. Every step is required or it shouldn't be a step.
- Mascot quotes should rotate slowly enough that you don't notice the change — never under 10 seconds.
- Recursive folder UIs need a hover affordance separate from the selected state.
